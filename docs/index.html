About PocketClaw

PocketClaw is a native iOS client for OpenClaw, an open-source AI assistant platform. It connects to your self-hosted OpenClaw server over WebSocket, giving you access to your AI agents, skills, and scheduled tasks from anywhere.

Getting Started

  • Set up an OpenClaw server on your own machine or server
  • Open PocketClaw and enter your server's WebSocket URL (e.g. wss://your-server:18789)
  • Authenticate with your gateway token or password
  • Start chatting with your AI agents

Don't have a server? Tap the terminal icon on the welcome screen 5 times to try the built-in demo mode.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is an open-source AI assistant that you can self-host. It supports multiple AI agents, skills, scheduled tasks, and integrations with platforms like Telegram and Discord.

Do I need my own server?

Yes. PocketClaw is a client that connects to your self-hosted OpenClaw server. You can try the app without a server using the built-in demo mode.

How do I connect?

Enter your server's WebSocket URL (starting with wss:// or ws://) and your authentication token or password on the setup screen.

Is my data stored on your servers?

No. PocketClaw connects directly to your own server. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. See our Privacy Policy.

The app can't connect to my server

Make sure your server is running and reachable from your device. If using a local network, ensure your phone is on the same network. For remote access, consider using a VPN or tunnel service like Tailscale.

What does demo mode do?

Demo mode lets you explore the app's interface with sample data. No server connection is needed. To activate it, tap the terminal icon on the welcome screen 5 times.
